Minneapolis Bike Groups

Join a group ride with a community of cyclists in Minneapolis. This is a list of groups that host regular rides throughout the season. Come make friends 😊

Notice something missing? Submit feedback or corrections here.

Angry Catfish Group Rides

Want to discover great local bike routes? We'd love to ride with you. Here you'll find shop rides, events, and fun biking things we're part of. Explore some of our favorites on our Strave Club page. Riding bikes is about the fun, the adventure, and the stories we’re left with when we get home. We hope you feel the same way.

Lots of different options for shop rides ranging from speedy to gravely to social.

💻 website

Biking with Baddies

Come bike with some Beautiful, Authentic, Divine, Determined, Intelligent, Energetic Sistas

🌅 instagram

Bone Saw Cycling Collective ⭐

Making cycling better for all by improving its members skill in any and all cycling disciplines, while fostering a non-judgmental atmosphere.

Bone Saw rides on Tuesdays throughout the spring, summer, and early fall. This group meets at the top of Martin Olav Sabo Bridge, and it’s one of the largest groups by ridership in the city. I’ve starred this group as really awesome, well organized, and safety-focused. Watch our on their social channels for additional trans / gender non-conforming rides.

🌅 instagram • 👥 facebook group • 🌲 linktree

Corpse Whale Racing

We're a Twin Cities-based team who race road, criteriums, cyclocross, endurance, and track

Racing team that also hosts regular social rides.

👥 facebook group • 🌅 instagram

Cycle Sisters

We host fun-ass themed bike rides while supporting Minneapolis businesses owned by women and BIPOC owners. BIPOC woman safe space. Open 2 all.

Get ride updates and notifications using the google form on their instagram page.

👥 facebook group • 🌅 instagram

Dangerous Man Adventure Rides

Dangerous Man is teaming up with @behindbarsbycicleshop for an exhilarating bicycle ride! A community bike ride and we will finish off with beers in the taproom! It’s a celebration of bikes, brews, and endless summer vibes.

Rides usually announced by the Behind Bars instagram account.

💻 website •📍Northeast

Fulton Social Series

Fulton Racing cordially invites everyone to join us on our no-drop #FultonSocialSeries rides on Thursday nights. First attendance earns you a free Fulton beer token.

👥 facebook group • 🌅 instagram •📍Northeast

Grease Rag

Grease Rag encourages and empowers FTW (Femme/Trans/Women, Non-binary, Two-spirit) cyclists in a collaborative and fun learning environment through rides, discussions, shop nights and educational seminars in a safer space.

💻 website • 🌅 instagram • 👥 facebook group •📍Seward

Freewheel Shop Rides

Regular rides that leave from various freewheel shops in the Twin Cities. Various days and pace groups available.

📅 Calendar •📍Multiple Locations

Joyful Riders Club

Monthly social rides at a friendly pace. Joyful Riders hosts DJ rides once a month.

🌅 instagram

Perennial Cycle Shop Rides

Perennial hosts and/or promotes all sorts of events. DJ rides, family fun rides, FTW rides, and the 30 Days of Biking pastry rides. Follow their instagram for event updates.

🌅 instagram •📍South Uptown

PieCycle Club

MPLS Piecycle Club is a Minneapolis based cycling club focused on building fun and inviting biking experiences for riders of all levels. We organize chill (Pastry Pace), family-friendly group rides around Minneapolis that start and/or end with delicious baked goods.

💻 website • 🌅 instagram • 📅 calendar

Plymouth Bikes & Beer

Medium to quick paced Thursday evening gravel rides that leave from Trailhead Plymouth and end at Luce Line Brewing.

👥 facebook group •📍Plymouth

Recover Bike Shop

Saturday Night Rides

Just a casual exploration of Northeast. No-drop pace. Expect about an hour of riding. We roll out from Recovery Bike Shop at 6:30. We're generally back at the shop around 8pm. Sometimes we get ice cream after.

Coffee (Outside)

Coffee (Outside) is our weekly excuse to start off the week on a bike. Everyone is always welcome. We’ll bring some treats to share. You bring you (and maybe a friend).

Details and other various rides shared on the shop instagram.

🌅 instagram • 📅 calendar •📍Northeast

Slow Roll MSP

Slow Roll Twin Cities aims to challenge negative perceptions of the city and foster economic revitalization.

👥 facebook group

Sociable Cider Werks Group Rides

A place for the riders of Sociable Cider Werks to connect with one another! Keep an eye on our social media pages for any announcements regarding weather cancellations.

👥 facebook group •📍Northeast

Stamina Racing Collective

Stamina Racing Collective is a FTW (femme/trans/women) cycling team based in Minneapolis, MN that is working to diversify the sport of cycling through mentorship, improved accessibility, and community development.

💻 website • 📅 calendar

Utepils Cycling

Beers. Bikes. Community. That’s the goal of Utepils Cycling Friends. Utepils Brewery is steps from over 15 miles of mountain bike trails and hundreds of miles of trails and routes for road cycling. We aim to create a place where cyclists can increase their skill and get out cycling in a relaxing and fun atmosphere.

All ride organization happens in the facebook group (below).

Utepils Women’s Wednesay Rides

Organized in the same facebook group.

👥 facebook group •📍Theodore Wirth Park

⛺ Bikepacking

A few groups in the twin cities lead bikepacking trips every year. These events are mostly advertised on social media and do tend to change from year to year.

Utepils, Fulton, Angry Catfish, and Thimbleweed have all organized trips in the past.

🚧 Critical Mass

Last Saturday of every month. Meet at 4PM, roll out at 5PM at “The Quarry” shopping center in NE. bit.ly/criticalmassmpls
